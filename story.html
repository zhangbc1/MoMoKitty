Meet MOMOKITTY, a half-fruit, half-cat elf born from the intersection of ancient legends and modern sweetness.

01 / The Origin

The intersection of two miracles.

A sweet, fluffy blend of a millennia-old Peachia star-peach and a lucky Japanese Mikeneko spirit. Her story begins in the distant Peachia Galaxy, a soft, warm world made of cotton candy clouds and peach juice rivers, where residents are natural "Happiness Collectors." MOMO was born with "eyes that find hidden happiness," a gentle soul on a sweet mission. In Peachia, a star-peach, laden with maximal "good luck energy" accumulated over ten thousand years, falls through time to find someone needing "a little sweet." It merges with a sleepy Mikeneko spirit, creating MOMO—a "fated embrace" across lightyears.

02 / The Magic

Every detail heals you.

MOMO destined journey first brought her to Kyoto, Japan, an ancient and refined land of beauty and precision. She came there for a three-year journey of detail cultivation. Kyoto, with its deep awareness of the seasons, its thoughtful way of life, and its aesthetic appreciation for small details, was the perfect place for her.There, MOMOKITTY did not simply collect beautiful things. She cultivated the soul of detail through the spirit of Kodawari—a devotion to care, refinement, and perfection in every small element. During this time, she learned how to blend the natural sweetness of Peachia with the timeless discipline of Japanese craftsmanship and attentiveness.She also embraced the classic symbolism of the lucky cat: her raised right paw invites good fortune, and her wink serves as a friendly little forecast of luck to come.Her core source of energy is her red collar, which holds two special charms. The Golden Bell, also called the Purity Bell, transforms stress into peach-flavored bubbles. The Green Leaf, an Amulet of Amplified Good Luck, magnifies Peachia’s sweet energy and acts as a detector for hidden fortune. Together, they form her personal amulet—delicate, meaningful, and perfectly complete.

03 / The Guardian

Your exclusive Koi Cat.

MOMO destined journey eventually brought her to California, a land full of sunshine, diversity, dreams, and action. Her mission there is to combine the refined sense of detail she cultivated in Japan with the free, open spirit of California, creating a unique kind of healing stop—the Peachia Healing Stop.MOMOKITTY does not simply bring products. She brings an entirely new lifestyle: Cal-Japanese Fusion. She believes that the power of cuteness and healing is more than a visual style—it is a positive, gentle, and joyful way of living. Her life in California is about creating a new daily rhythm, where detailed happiness and free-spirited sunshine come together. Through this, she offers a new kind of steady everyday companionship—warm, certain, and comforting.
